## App Review Information Response

**Copy this into the "App Review Information > Notes" section in App Store Connect:**

```
Re: Guideline 4.1(a) - Third-Party Content Reference

Thank you for your review. We have taken the following actions:

1. SCREENSHOTS UPDATED
   - Removed the home screen screenshot that displayed third-party apps
   - All screenshots now show only OneTapSafe features and interface
   - Added captions emphasizing our elderly/senior care focus

2. APP DIFFERENTIATION
   OneTapSafe is specifically designed for daily safety check-ins for elderly 
   and vulnerable individuals, with automatic emergency alerts to family members.

   Our target audience and use case are distinctly different from general 
   check-in apps:
   
   • Target Users: Seniors/elderly individuals and their adult children/caregivers
   • Primary Purpose: Daily wellness verification with missed check-in alerts
   • Core Value: Peace of mind for families caring for aging parents
   • KEY TECHNICAL DIFFERENTIATOR: iOS Live Activity on lock screen - users can 
     check in with a single tap WITHOUT unlocking their device. This is critical 
     for elderly users who may struggle with Face ID, remembering passcodes, or 
     navigating to apps.
   • Advanced Feature: Family emergency notifications when check-ins are missed

3. TERMINOLOGY CLARIFICATION
   The term "tap" is common in iOS app UX language and describes the standard 
   gesture for interacting with touchscreen interfaces. It is not proprietary 
   to any specific app.

   Our app name "OneTapSafe" refers to the simplified one-button interface 
   designed specifically for elderly users who may have difficulty with 
   complex apps.

4. METADATA UPDATED
   We have updated our App Store description and keywords to clearly 
   communicate our elder care focus and differentiate from general-purpose apps.

We believe these changes clearly demonstrate OneTapSafe's unique value 
proposition serving the elderly care market, distinct from other apps in 
the safety/check-in category.

Thank you for your consideration.
```
